CS1200: What did I learn from this class?

Resume + Cover Letter

During CS1200, we looked through potential mock internships and created a resume listing our achievements and skills and wrote a cover letter based off of the internship that we found online. I found this assignment useful since I learned that employers actually only scan your resume for about a minute before moving onto someone else's resume. In addition, I was also able to obtain feedback from my peers regarding how to better present myself to employers from the PowerPoint presentation shown in class.
